# Welcome

The **CoverGo Platform** is a configurable, modular platform that gives you the building blocks every business application needs — identity, data, rules, workflows, communications, documents, and more — ready to compose into the solution your business actually needs.

Whether you're setting things up for the first time or you're an everyday user looking to get a specific task done, you'll find clear, task-oriented answers here.

{% hint style="success" %}
**New here?** Jump straight to the [Quick start](#quick-start) below and you'll be productive in minutes.
{% endhint %}

## Who this guide is for

This documentation is written for everyone who works inside the platform:

* **Administrators** who configure the platform — managing users, roles, data models, rules, workflows, communications, and more.
* **Business users** who use the platform day-to-day — working with parties, running processes, and the work that keeps the business running.
* **Power users** who want to push the platform further — designing complex rule sets, orchestrating workflows, and tailoring how the system behaves.

You don't need to be technical. The platform is designed to be configured by business users, and this guide is written the same way.

## Quick start

Whether you've just been invited to the platform or you've been handed the keys as an admin, here's the shortest path to value.

{% hint style="info" %}
**You're a new user?** Start at step 1.\
**You're a new administrator?** Start at step 1, then continue through step 4.
{% endhint %}

### 1. Sign in for the first time

Open the link your administrator sent you and set your password. → [Authentication](todo-authentication/README.md)

### 2. Find your way around

Take a quick tour of the navigation, search, and your personal settings. The shape of the platform mirrors the chapters of this guide.

### 3. Do your first task

Pick the capability that brought you here — managing users, configuring a rule, composing a document, sending a notification — and follow its **how-to** page. Each capability has a short walk-through built for first-timers.

### 4. _(Admins)_ Set up your team

Create user groups, assign roles, and invite teammates. Once the team is in, configure the data model, rules, workflows, and notifications that power your business. → [Identity and Access](todo-identity-and-access/README.md)
